Have a question? Check out the FAQ

What is Telecom Invoicing System (TIS)?

Telecom Invoicing System is the process of managing and controlling an organization’s telecommunications services and related costs. This includes voice, data, and mobile services, as well as the infrastructure, devices, and contracts that support them.

Why Is TIS Important For My Business?

Implementing TIS can significantly reduce your telecom costs, often one of the largest operational expenses for businesses with extensive telecom needs. TIS ensures you are not overpaying for services, helps identify billing errors, and optimizes your telecom services to match your actual business requirements. This results in cost savings and more efficient use of your telecom resources.

How Can TIS Save Money For My Company?

TIS saves money by preventing overcharges through invoice validation, ensuring contract compliance, and eliminating unnecessary services. Regular reviews and optimizations of your telecom environment, as part of TIS, also uncover new savings opportunities by aligning services with current needs and market conditions.

What Services Are Included In TIS Offerings?

TIS services include end-to-end billing automation, inventory management, service usage analysis, contract management, dispute resolution, and management reporting of move, add, change, and disconnected lines. Each service is designed to handle specific aspects of telecom expense management and can be tailored to meet the unique needs of your organization.

Can TIS Be Customized For My Specific Industry Or Business Size?

Yes, TIS services are highly customizable to fit the specific needs of different industries and companies of all sizes. Whether you operate in a sector like healthcare, finance, or education, or you're a small business or a large enterprise, our solutions can be tailored to address your specific challenges and objectives.
